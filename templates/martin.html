Martingale Betting Simulator

"If you lose, just double the bet!"

This app can show you the result of the martingale strategy in a "coinflip game".
The coinflip game means that you have 50%-50% chance of doubling or losing your money.
You can set your starting money, the number of rounds (i.e. the number of flips) and the number of games you want to simulate.
After hitting "Play!", a graph will appear showing your pot over time.
You can go into negative with your money just to illustrate how much you can lose before getting back to positive again.
